Death Tips

Unfortunately, you have just died. The good news is that if your character is level 10 or under you have revived with no experience loss.

This will not be the case after you reach 11th level. At that time you will being to lose a small amount of experience as a result of dying, and you can have your body resurrected to restore some of that lost experience.

If your character is high enough level to leave a corpse, it will remain in the game for 24 hours. After that time it will "decay" and disappear from the world.

- To help you find your corpse, if you have time, type /loc to get your location before you die.
- To help you find your corpse, Bards, Necromancer and Shadowknights have spells that locate and even summon corpses. It's a good idea to offer a reward for such services, even if you have to get that reward off your corpse.

For more information, please see Help.
